## Summary

Planefence states that it is designed not to collect, store, or process personal information under GDPR, CCPA/CPRA, and LGPD definitions.

## Data Practices Echo (from `privacy.json` / `README-privacy.md`)

- No collection of names, email addresses, phone numbers, postal addresses, account/login details, linked IP addresses, online identifiers, location data, or payment/transaction data
- No analytics, advertising cookies, or tracking pixels for cross-time user tracking
- No selling or sharing of personal data
- No profiling or automated decision-making based on personal data

## Browser-Local Preference Data

The policy states that limited preference data may be stored in browser local storage/cookies, including:

- Theme preference (dark/light)
- Last viewed page (`planefence` or `plane-alert`)
- Language preference
- Consent state (if provided)

The policy states these values are for user experience and are not sent as personal data.

## Operator Responsibility

The privacy text states that operators deploying Planefence are responsible for their own compliance and runtime behavior.

## Controls

Users can control local storage/cookies in browser settings and via on-site controls (if provided).
